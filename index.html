Naruto : I dream is to be become a hokage

My name is Naruto Uzumaki. I am a ninja from the Hidden Leaf Village. I have a dream to become the Hokage, the leader of my village. I want to be recognized and respected by everyone.

My journey has been tough, but I have made many friends along the way. I have learned the importance of teamwork and never giving up. I will continue to train hard and strive to achieve my dream.
